Hot-Spinach Artichoke

Amazing Hot-Spinach Artichoke Recipe

This spinach artichoke dip is delicious. It's so cheesy and fragrant. If you don't like artichokes, don't worry — you'll never know they're in there!

My only question is: Is it okay to eat it with a spoon right out of the dish?

Ingredients:

  1. 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  2. ¼ cup mayonnaise
  3. ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  4. ¼ cup grated Romano cheese
  5. 1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
  6. ½ teaspoon dried basil
  7. ¼ teaspoon garlic salt
  8. salt and pepper to taste
  9. 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  10. ½ cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  11. ¼ cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  • STEP 1:

    • Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a small baking dish.

  • STEP 2:

    • Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, Romano cheese, garlic, basil, garlic salt, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.

  • STEP 3:

    • Gently stir in artichoke hearts and spinach.

  • STEP 4:

    • Transfer the mixture to the prepared baking dish; top with mozzarella cheese.

  • STEP 5:

    • Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and lightly browned, about 25 minutes.
